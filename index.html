About Us

Welcome to Sinai University Dorms , SU provides dorms for students to live in for each semester until they graduate. The rooms are fully finished and furnished,The dorms allow our students to be on campus 24/7 and are only a few walking minutes away from their classes. All dorms include AC and a study corner within the room.

Of course, it’s up to the student to have a room on their own, or a shared room with one other colleague or two. Thank you for choosing SU Dorms as your preferred SU Dorms app. We're here to make your journey as smooth and convenient as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Submit your questions to us.

  • What is SU Dorms?

    SU Dorms is a Housing booking app that helps users plan and book their room more efficiently and conveniently.

  • Is SU Dorms available for both iOS and Android devices?

    Yes, SU Dorms is available for both iOS and Android platforms.

  • Does SU Dorms require an internet connection?

    Yes, SU Dorms requires an internet connection to function. This is because the app uses real-time data to provide the latest information on room schedules, prices, and availability.

  • Is the payment process secure on TripTo?

    Yes, the payment process on SU Dorms is secure and encrypted. Your personal and financial information is protected by industry-standard security protocols.

  • What if I need to cancel or change my booking?

    SU Dorms offers an easy cancellation and change policy. You can make changes to your booking directly from the app, and any cancellations will be subject to the terms and conditions specified by the dorms company.
